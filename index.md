## About me {#about}

Hello! I'm a Bioengineering PhD student at the University of Pennsylvania. I'm curious about biological intelligence.

I use deep learning, dynamical system, and information theory tools to study sleep neuroscience. I also explore the integration and connections between computational methods and biological neural systems. My PhD advisor is Prof. Franz Weber.
